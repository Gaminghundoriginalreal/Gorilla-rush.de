Privacy Policy

for the game Fart Tag

1. Overview

Welcome to Fart Tag! We take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy outlines what data we collect and how we use it.

2. IP Address Logging

We log the IP addresses of players to help prevent abuse, cheating, or other violations of our community rules. This data is used strictly for security and moderation purposes, including banning users if necessary.

3. Use of PlayFab

Fart Tag uses PlayFab as a backend service for player account management, data storage, and in-game currency. PlayFab may collect user-related data as part of their platform. For more information, please review PlayFab’s own privacy policy.

4. Use of Photon

We use Photon for real-time multiplayer networking. Photon may process your connection data such as IP address and region for matchmaking and latency optimization.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties, except for the necessary services mentioned above (PlayFab and Photon).

6. Your Rights

If you wish to request deletion of your data or have any privacy-related questions, feel free to contact us.

7. Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
